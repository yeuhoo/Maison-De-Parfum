# Maison De Parfum UI Kit Specification (Developer-Ready)

## 1. Design Principles

Warm luxury, minimal elegance, editorial spacing, high perceived value, calm aesthetic.  
Rules: generous white space, no heavy shadows, no glossy gradients, no cool tones, no loud promotional styling.

## 2. Color System

**Primary Colors:**

- Champagne Gold #C6A75E – Primary buttons and accents
- Warm Ivory #F6F1E8 – Main background
- Soft Cream #EDE3D3 – Section background
- Deep Espresso #2B2622 – Primary text
- Matte Black #111111 – High contrast

**Supporting Neutrals:** Warm Taupe #B8A999, Muted Sand #D8C6AF, Bridal White #FAF8F5, Blush Nude #E7D5C8

**Usage Ratio:** 60% Ivory, 25% neutrals, 10% gold, 5% black

## 3. Typography

**Primary Heading Font:** Playfair Display (fallback: Georgia)  
**Body Font:** Montserrat (fallback: Arial)

**Type Scale:** H1 56px, H2 40px, H3 28px, Body 16–18px, Button 14px uppercase

**Font Weights:** 400 and 600 for headings; 400 for body; 500 for buttons

## 4. Spacing & Grid

8px spacing system. Section padding 96–120px desktop, 48–64px mobile.  
Max width 1320px. 12-column grid desktop, 4-column mobile.

## 5. Buttons

**Primary Button:** Background #C6A75E, White text, 4px radius, hover #B8974E.  
**Secondary Button:** Transparent, 1px gold border, gold text.  
**Text Button:** Underline on hover.

## 6. Forms

Input height 52px, 1px solid #D8C6AF border, 4px radius.  
Focus: 1px solid #C6A75E. No glow effects.  
Error: 1px muted red border with 14px helper text.

## 7. Enquiry Form – Wedding Perfume Bar

**Form Purpose:** Capture qualified wedding leads for consultation and quotation.

**Required Fields:**  
• Full Name (Required)  
• Email Address (Required)  
• Guest Count (Required – numeric field)  
• Date of Event (Required – date selector)

**Optional Field:**  
• Venue Address (Optional – text field)

**CTA Button:** 'Request Consultation' (Primary Gold Button)

**Post-Submission:** Thank you confirmation message with next-step expectation (response within 24–48 hours).

## 8. Cards

**Product Cards:** Soft cream background, subtle hover lift.  
**Wedding Package Cards:** Gold 'Most Popular' tag option.

## 9. Navigation

80px height, sticky on scroll. Transparent over hero, ivory after scroll.  
Menu text uppercase Montserrat 14px. Gold hover state.

## 10. Footer

Ivory background, thin sand divider, newsletter signup with gold button.

## 11. Image Direction

Warm lighting, soft shadows, linen/marble textures, bridal florals.  
Avoid cool lighting and busy backgrounds.

## 12. Animation

0.3s ease-in-out transitions. Fade and subtle slide only. No bounce effects.
